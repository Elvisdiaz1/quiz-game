Coding Quiz Game

Welcome to the coding quiz game! Try to answer the following questions within the time limit. The next question will appear when you choose the correct answer. When you answer all the questions, your score will be the time left on the timer and you can save the score to complete against your friends or aim to get a better score. If you choose the incorrect answer, your time limit will decrease. If you do not answer all the questions in time, you lose. Try your best and have fun! (You may need to hit the choices two times as the quiz is still a little buggy)
